ABOUT

This site is a tool for fantasy TTRPG (tabletop roleplaying games) narrators. Often those narrators needs to roleplay some NPCs (non-playable characters) for the players to interact with. Almost every time that NPCs are made on the fly and lacks of flavor. With NPCrafter you can create, edit and have ready your NPCs to bring them during the playthrough.
